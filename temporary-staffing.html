What is Temporary Staffing?

Temporary staffing is a flexible employment model where workers are hired for a fixed duration — ranging from a few days to several months. These staff members remain on JMD Consultancy's payroll while working at your location, handling your operational tasks.

This model is ideal when you need additional workforce for specific projects, seasonal demands, employee absences (like maternity leave), or when you want to evaluate candidates before offering permanent roles.

  • Zero Hiring Hassle
  • No Payroll Burden
  • Immediate Availability
  • Scale Up or Down Flexibly

When Do You Need Temporary Staffing?

Common scenarios where temporary staffing is the smartest workforce strategy.

Seasonal Peaks

Festive seasons, year-end, or tax season — ramp up your workforce for 2-3 months and scale down after the peak passes.

Example: E-commerce festive sale
Specific Projects

Short-term projects like data migration, surveys, or new product launches that need additional manpower temporarily.

Example: 3-month digitization project
Employee Absences

Cover for maternity leave, long sick leave, sabbaticals, or sudden resignations without disrupting operations.

Example: 6-month maternity cover
Try-Before-Hire

Evaluate candidates on the job before offering permanent positions. Reduce permanent hiring risks.

Example: 3-month temp-to-perm
Events & Exhibitions

Staff for trade shows, conferences, product launches, and promotional events for a few days.

Example: 3-day trade show
New Location Setup

Quick workforce deployment for new branch/office setup while permanent hiring is in progress.

Example: New city expansion
