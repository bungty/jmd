What is End-to-End Hiring?

End-to-End Hiring (E2E Hiring) is a comprehensive recruitment solution where JMD Consultancy manages the entire hiring process on your behalf — from understanding your workforce needs and sourcing candidates to conducting interviews, negotiating offers, and ensuring smooth onboarding.

This approach eliminates the burden on your internal HR team, reduces time-to-fill, and ensures you get the best-fit candidates for every role.

  • 100% Process Ownership
  • Dedicated Account Manager
  • Pan India Talent Reach
  • Quality & Speed Assured

End-to-End Recruitment Process

Our 7-Step End-to-End Hiring Process

A structured, transparent, and efficient recruitment methodology to deliver the best talent.

1. Requirement Gathering

Deep-dive discussion to understand job roles, skills, experience, culture fit, and urgency.

2. Sourcing Strategy

Multi-channel sourcing using job portals, social media, database, and referrals.

3. Screening & Shortlisting

Resume screening, telephonic interviews, and skill assessments for shortlisting.

4. Interview Coordination

Scheduling interviews between candidates and hiring managers seamlessly.

5. Offer Management

Managing offer rollout, negotiations, and handling candidate queries.

6. Background Check

Verification of documents, employment history, and references.

7. Onboarding Support

Ensuring smooth joining, documentation, and initial integration.
