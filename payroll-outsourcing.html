What is Payroll Outsourcing?

Payroll outsourcing is the process of delegating your entire payroll operations to an external expert — JMD Consultancy. We take over salary calculations, statutory deductions (PF, ESI, PT, TDS), compliance filings, payslip generation, and all related administrative tasks.

This eliminates the need for an in-house payroll team, reduces errors and compliance risks, and ensures your employees are paid accurately and on time — every single month. Our team stays constantly updated with changing labour laws and tax regulations across all Indian states.
