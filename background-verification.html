What is Background Verification?

Background Verification (BGV) is the process of validating the information provided by a candidate — including their identity, employment history, educational qualifications, criminal records, and other credentials. It's a critical step in the hiring process to ensure you're bringing trustworthy individuals into your organization.

JMD Consultancy provides comprehensive BGV services with fast turnaround times, thorough checks, and detailed verification reports. We help companies across India reduce hiring fraud, comply with industry regulations, and build a safe, reliable workforce.

Our Verification Process

A fast, transparent, and confidential 5-step verification process.

1
Candidate Consent

We obtain written consent from the candidate as per legal requirements before initiating checks.

2
Document Collection

Collection of all relevant documents — ID proofs, certificates, relieving letters, references.

3
Verification Execution

Multi-source verification through database checks, institution/employer contact, and physical visits.

Report Generation
