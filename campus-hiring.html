What is Campus Hiring?

Campus hiring is a strategic recruitment approach where companies visit educational institutions to hire fresh graduates and post-graduates. JMD Consultancy acts as your complete campus recruitment partner — identifying the right colleges, managing pre-placement talks, conducting assessments, and coordinating the entire selection process.

This approach builds a strong talent pipeline for entry-level roles, reduces long-term recruitment costs, and brings fresh perspectives and energy into your organization.

Our Campus Hiring Process

A structured 6-step approach to hire the best fresh talent from top institutions.

1
College Identification

We shortlist colleges based on your role requirements, skill sets, location preferences, and budget.

2
Pre-Placement Engagement

Organizing pre-placement talks, webinars, and branding sessions to attract top students.

3
Registration & Screening

Managing student registrations and initial resume screening based on eligibility criteria.

4
Online Assessments

Aptitude tests, technical assessments, and psychometric evaluations conducted online or on-campus.

5
Interview Coordination

Scheduling and managing technical, group discussion, and HR interview rounds seamlessly.

6
Offer Rollout & Onboarding

Managing offer letters, acceptance tracking, and pre-joining engagement until onboarding.

Why Campus Hiring Makes Business Sense

Strategic advantages of building a fresher talent pipeline for your organization.

Fresh Perspectives

Young graduates bring innovative ideas, new energy, and adaptability to evolving business needs.

Cost-Effective Hiring

Lower salary expectations compared to experienced hires. Build talent from within at reduced costs.

Strong Employer Branding

Campus presence builds long-term brand recognition among the future workforce and academic circles.

Bulk Hiring at Speed

Hire multiple candidates in a single drive. Ideal for filling large batches of entry-level positions quickly.

Higher Retention Rates

Freshers trained within your culture tend to stay longer and grow into loyal, long-term employees.

Succession Planning

Build a strong talent bench for future leadership roles by hiring and nurturing graduates early.
