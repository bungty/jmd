What is HR Process Outsourcing?

HR Process Outsourcing (HRPO) involves delegating routine and complex HR tasks to JMD Consultancy. We handle everything from payroll and compliance to employee records and attendance management, ensuring your HR operations run smoothly while you focus on strategic business goals.

Whether you are a startup with no dedicated HR team or an established company looking to reduce administrative burden, our scalable solutions fit your needs perfectly.

  • Reduce Operational Costs
  • Ensure Statutory Compliance
  • Access HR Expertise
  • Minimize Administrative Errors

HR Functions We Manage

Comprehensive HR outsourcing covering the entire employee lifecycle administration.

Payroll Management

Accurate salary calculations, tax deductions, reimbursements, and timely payouts with full compliance to statutory norms like PF, ESI, and TDS.

Compliance & Legal

Stay compliant with labour laws, shop & establishment acts, minimum wages, and all statutory filings. We handle audits and inspections.

Attendance & Leave

Biometric integration, attendance tracking, leave policy management, and automated alerts for irregularities or absenteeism.

Employee Data Management

Centralized digital records for all employees including personal files, documents, appraisals, and history securely maintained.

Offer & Appointment Letters

Generation of standard and customized offer letters, appointment orders, confirmation letters, and experience certificates.

HR Analytics & Reporting

Monthly HR dashboards, attrition reports, salary registers, and compliance reports for management review.
