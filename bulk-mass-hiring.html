What is Bulk & Mass Hiring?

Bulk or Mass Hiring refers to recruiting a large number of candidates — typically 50 to 5000+ — within a short timeframe. This is common when companies launch new projects, expand to new cities, set up new branches, or have seasonal staffing needs.

JMD Consultancy specializes in managing high-volume recruitment with speed, precision, and quality. We handle everything from sourcing and screening to interview coordination and offer rollout — all while maintaining consistent candidate quality across locations.
