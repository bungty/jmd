What is Permanent Hiring?

Permanent hiring is the process of recruiting candidates for long-term, core positions within your organization. These employees become part of your permanent workforce, contribute to your company's growth, and build long-term careers with you.

At JMD Consultancy, we don't just fill positions — we find the right people who align with your company culture, values, and long-term vision. Our permanent recruitment process emphasizes quality over quantity, thorough screening, and cultural fit assessment to ensure high retention and performance.
