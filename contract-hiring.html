What is Contract Hiring?

Contract hiring is a staffing model where professionals are engaged for a fixed duration — typically 6 months to 2 years — to work on specific projects, fill skill gaps, or manage temporary workload increases. These contract employees remain on JMD Consultancy's payroll while working at your location or remotely.

Unlike temporary staffing which is often for shorter durations and general roles, contract hiring focuses on skilled and semi-skilled professionals for longer, more specialized assignments. It's the perfect solution when you need expertise without permanent overhead.

Our Contract Hiring Models

Flexible engagement models to suit different business needs and project requirements.

Fixed-Term Contract

Staff engaged for a pre-defined period (6, 12, 18, or 24 months). Ideal for projects with clear timelines and deliverables. Contract ends automatically on the specified date.

Most Popular
Project-Based Contract

Staff hired for the duration of a specific project. The contract is tied to project completion rather than a calendar date. Perfect for IT implementations, migrations, etc.

Flexible Duration
Contract-to-Hire

Start with a contract period (typically 6 months) and convert to permanent employment if the candidate proves to be a great fit. Reduces permanent hiring risks significantly.
